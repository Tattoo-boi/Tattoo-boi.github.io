About the Project

This project is a creative representation of an imagined alien encounter in the New York City subway. Combining animations, visuals, and sound, it brings the mystery of urban alien sightings to life.

All images were sourced from Google, and the sound effects were inspired by ambient subway noises.

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • p5.js for animations
  • Photoshop for the main image
